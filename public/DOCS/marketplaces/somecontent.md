Module: Public Marketplace Experience

1. Overview

The Marketplace is the public-facing experience of PlotHub and the primary touchpoint for prospective buyers. It must do far more than display property listings—it should build trust, reduce uncertainty, and guide visitors toward confident decisions.

Unlike traditional classified platforms, PlotHub's marketplace should feel like a professional digital product. Every page should answer the buyer's questions, reinforce vendor credibility, and make it easy to take the next step.

The marketplace should be:

Trust-first
Search-driven
Mobile-first
Fast and responsive
Accessible
SEO-optimized
Conversion-focused
Consistent across all pages
2. Marketplace Goals

The marketplace should enable visitors to:

Discover relevant land and plots.
Compare different options.
Understand pricing and ownership details.
Evaluate vendor credibility.
Save properties for later.
Share listings.
Request site visits.
Contact vendors securely.
Begin a purchase journey with confidence.

22. Marketplace Design Principles

Every marketplace page should adhere to these principles:

Trust before conversion: verification, transparency, and clear information should be more prominent than sales messaging.
Content hierarchy: the most important decisions (price, location, verification, and actions) should always be visible without overwhelming the user.
Progressive disclosure: present essential information first, with deeper details available as users explore.
Consistency: shared layouts, cards, filters, and action patterns should behave the same across the platform.
Performance: optimize images, lazy-load non-critical content, and maintain fast interactions.
Accessibility: keyboard navigation, readable typography, sufficient contrast, and descriptive labels should be standard.
