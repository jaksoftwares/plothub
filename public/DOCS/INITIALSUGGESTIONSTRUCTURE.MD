Trust Indicators (Persistent)

To reinforce credibility throughout the experience, include a lightweight trust strip beneath the hero or in strategic locations on marketing pages. It can feature metrics and assurances such as:

Verified Vendors
Verified Listings
Secure Enquiries
Transparent Pricing
Professional Support

Accompany these with live platform statistics where available, for example:

1,200+ Verified Properties
85+ Trusted Vendors
15,000+ Monthly Visitors
2,500+ Site Visits Scheduled

These figures should come from real data once the platform is live, but the layout should be designed from the beginning to accommodate them. They reinforce the platform's core promise of trust without overwhelming the interface.

My Recommended Public Navigation

After considering the full product vision, I would keep the top navigation intentionally focused:

Left

Logo
Browse
Map
Vendors
Resources
Pricing

Right

Search
Wishlist (authenticated)
Login
Get Started (primary CTA)

Everything else—including About, Blog, Careers, Legal, FAQs, Support, and Contact—belongs in the footer or within the Resources menu. This keeps the header uncluttered, prioritizes the two primary user journeys (discover land and become a vendor), and leaves room for the platform to grow without constantly redesigning the navigation
